Be it utensils or clothes, washing is a part of daily chores in every household and other places for cleanliness. We, Padam Products, being a prominent Manufacturer and Exporter, offer a wide range of detergent and washing products including Washing Powder, Laundry Powder, Detergent Soap, Detergent Bar etc. Results of washing process highly depends on the quality of the cleansing agent we use. Thus, we focus on the quality products to serve our clients with the best.

We supply skin friendly detergents made from standard quality raw materials to give desired washing results. With the quality assured, we are meeting the requirements of cleansing products of domestic as well as international clients. For this, we have a well equipped infrastructure with trained team to support our organization's smooth working.
